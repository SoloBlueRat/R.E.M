What genre is this?
1. Alternative Rock. Started in 1970 as underground rock and became popular in the 1990s. Their mainstream albums were Out of Time and Automatic for the people.
This genre would be considered American Underground movement in 1980s. The R-E-M band combined Guitar hooks and D.I.Y aesthetic borrowed from post-punk.

Was R-E-M a political movement band?
7. They are a political band movement towards to liberal and progressive views. One reaction about Conservative enviroment in the 1980s under Ronald Reagen.It highlights social and political issues, involving campaigns to Enviromental, human rights, and feminist movements, and encouraging voter registration. Despite Michael Stipe main lead of the group, the other band members were on "The same page" sharing politcal beliefs. However, some diagreements involved Stipe for animal treatment rights in 1990, while the rest of the band were not.
